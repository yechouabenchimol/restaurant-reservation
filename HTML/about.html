About Us

In few Word's

We are an online restaurant table reservation site, launched in November 2022 by Yechoua Benchimol and Chmouel Abergel. We are specialists in online booking with an innovative, clean, simple and above all ad-free site. We collaborate with many restaurants in Jerusalem and we hope to collaborate soon with restaurants throughout Israel. Our priorities are to provide a practical and quick solution to reserve a table in a restaurant without taking the time to call the restaurant. But also we want to help restaurants to have a better management of customers and organization while saving time by reducing calls concerning reservations.
